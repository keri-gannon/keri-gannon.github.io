Keri Gannon

Hello, my name is Keri. I grew up in the Chicago area. I studied French and teaching at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I lived in Paris for a year during college, which made me fall even more in love with the language, fashion, beauty, architecture, interior design, and food. I taught French in the Chicago area for three years, and then have been working as a software engineer ever since I started learning how to code. I have always been ambitious, self-disciplined, and focused, and I am so grateful for all of the wonderful people I've met and the experiences I've had in my life.

I am committed to living a life that feels fulfilling and following my passions. I live for love, beauty, art, family, friends, intellectual pursuits, and making the world a better place. Relationships are the most important thing to me.

I love to knit, sew, do make-up, bake, cook, decorate, paint, write, sing, play music, and do yoga. I love being creative. I hope to have enough time, energy, and money to continue pursuing all of the many passions I have in life.

I believe in being compassionate, empathetic, and kind to people. Life always has its challenges, and I believe that we should all be there for each other. I believe in supporting people in my life, being a good listener, and always encouraging them and cheering them on.
